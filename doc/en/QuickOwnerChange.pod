=head1 NAME

QuickOwnerChange - change the ticket owner quickly

=head1 DESCRIPTION

With this addon you get a dropdown in the ticket zoom and ticket overview that
makes it possible to change the owner with two clicks...

=head1 ACKNOWLEDGMENT

Thanks to all who contributed bugfixes or features:

=over 4

Thomtux added the possibility to automatically lock the ticket for the new
owner.

=back

=over 4

HYDRO Systems KG L<http://www.hydro.aero/> sponsored the feature that the responsible
